buy artworks

With our web service you can buy masterpieces right from the art exhibition!

How it works?
Come on, it's a child's play!
  1. Visit an art exhibition our service supported
  2. Find an artwork of your dream
  3. Open this website on your phone
  4. Using QR scanner tool you see in the left pane and camera on your phone, scan QR-code from artwork's sticker
  5. You'll be redirected to the webpage where you can make a purchase - all on place!
  6. Now this artwork is yours. Enjoy!
